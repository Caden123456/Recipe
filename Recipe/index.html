Get a large pot and fill it with water and a handful of salt and place it on the stove and set the temperature high to boil



Take the meat and put it into a large pan and place it on the stove and set the temperature to medium



Break the meat up into small pieces and flip the meat occasionally for around 10-15 minutes



Add all of the marinara sauce from the jar into the pan with the meat and mix it together and turn the heat down to low and let it simmer



Once water has boiled, place spaghetti into the water for around 7-11 minutes



Use tongs to drain the water from the noodles



Add noodles to the pasta and mix them together



Serve and enjoy
